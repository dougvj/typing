Several open source DRI drivers have been written, including ones for ATI
Mach64, ATI Rage128, ATI Radeon, 3dfx Voodoo3 through Voodoo5, Matrox G200
through G400, SiS 300-series, Intel i810 through i965, S3 Savage, VIA UniChrome
graphics chipsets, and nouveau for Nvidia. Some graphics vendors have written
closed-source DRI drivers, including Nvidia[citation needed], ATI, and Kyro.
The DRI is supported on Linux, OpenSolaris, FreeBSD, NetBSD, and OpenBSD.
